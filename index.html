Welcome To VAT Calculator (UK)

This site was created to help users calculate VAT accurately, whether adding it to net amounts or subtracting it from gross amounts. Unlike other tools, ours lets you adjust the VAT rate easily to suit your needs.

The UK’s standard VAT rate is currently 20%, a change made on 4th January 2011 from 17.5%. Previously, the rate dropped to 15% on 1st December 2008 before rising again to 17.5% on 20th January 2010. Our calculator is designed to simplify these calculations for both individuals and businesses.
